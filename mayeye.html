VEMAN JADHAV

MAY EYE: Take Your Order

Role: Programmer.
Year: 2022.
Genre:.
Platform: PC.

Description
Play as MAY EYE, a highly advanced artificially intelligent
storemanager as you manage three self-automated shopping carts
who need to fulfil shopping lists that are made by customers.

A proof-of-concept was submitted to the BYOG 2022 Game Jam.

The full game is available on itch.io for free.
