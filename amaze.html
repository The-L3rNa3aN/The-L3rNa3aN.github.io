VEMAN JADHAV

aMaze


Role: Designer and programmer.
Year: 2020.
Genre: Puzzle.
Platform: PC.

Description
A top down puzzle game which challenges your navigational skills
as you twist and turn in order to find the exit. With your only
source of help being the bright ground, can you pass all levels
bedecked with black, confusing obstructions?

Now available on itch.io for free.
